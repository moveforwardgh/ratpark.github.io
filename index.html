Welcome to Ratpark

A PLACE IN WHICH NATURE AND TECH MERGE

Is an intentional activist community placed in the jungles of Costa Rica, that has the objective to function as a co-developing space oriented to alternative social and technological innovation. Beyond social innovation, as an integral place, it's also intended to be an open dynamic place for different kinds of people, interests, and expertises. In that sense, it is a place in which community, activism, technology, and nature merge; towards collaboration in trying to contribute to create a better society and improve as human beings

Learn more

The installations

The place is located close to La Cabanga, a humble and kind village near Lake Arenal in the northern Costa Rica. It is nested in a jungle environment with diverse species around; surrounded by volcanoes, waterfalls, lakes and hot rivers.

It was built to be comfortable, biomimetic, minimalist, and aesthetically pleasing. The main space consists of a 500sqm building; to sleep it has two dorms, five private rooms and two cabins; aslo a 100 sqm octagonal movement space available for different activities.

Purpose

Socio-tech innovation

Is intended to function as a hub for pro-equality and regenerative social-technological innovation. Oriented towards topics like alternative economics, participative governance, positive technology and novel trains of thought in general

Trans-personal growth

As an integral community, its also meant to be a place for trans-personal growth, in which the self unites and blends with the collective; by co-exploring different ways of living, sharing, improving and healing respectively

Events space

It is meant to host related events that will take place frequently; like conferences, international meetings or courses. Also is to open to other external topics or projects that want to organize events

How it all started

The place was built with materials from the surroundings, with the least environmental impact possible; and was mounted in one year hand-by-hand by managers, international volunteers, and local workers jointly

The spaces

The place is composed of many different articulated spaces; that were designed and built for achieving efficiency, being comfortable, and sustainable

Co-work space

Common building

Integrated with off-grid electricity, high-speed internet, and enough place for lots of people to work at the same time

Kitchen

Common building

Fully equipped, with wooden and gas-based stoves and ovens, refrigerator, and big tables to eat jointly

Gardens

Outside

Permaculture-designed gardens, intended to provide healthy and tasty food with innovative cropping methods

Bedrooms

Secondary building

Many shared cabins, private cabins, and common-room mezzanines; built for achieving conformable resting

Octagon

Elevated structure

A octagonal movement space that is available for different activities like yoga, meditation, workouts, etc.

Bathrooms

Secondary building

Comfortable biomimetic and organic bathrooms, designed for avoiding waste and damaging the environment around
